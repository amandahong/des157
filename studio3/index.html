WELCOME TO MY BAG!

⊗

PENS PENS PENS

Maybe it's because I lose them a lot, or because my calligraphy mood is constantly changing, but I always have tons and tons of pens with me in my bag. I usually carry a few pens for notes, a few for sketching, a pencil for test taking, and a nice brush pen for doodling in my notebook!

⊗

CUSTOM NOTEBOOK

This notebook is special because it was custom made for me! It's a Moleskin that's been hand-embossed by an Instagram calligrapher named Eunice. I use this notebook for taking notes occasionally, but usually it becomes my go-to place for random scribbles.

⊗

CHARGER ORGANIZER

I own one of those obscenely long Mac charger cables, and this cord-wrap organizer is my favorite accessory of life. It keeps my cords coiled up under a silicone cover until I need to unravel them, so my charger is always customized to the length I need, and neat in my bag!

⊗

TEA PACKETS

I LOVE TEA. I always carry a thermos, and since hot water is free on campus, having a few tea bags stashed in my bag ensures that I can always have a hot drink ready to go, for FREE. My favorite flavor is genmaicha, which translates to roasted rice green tea. Yuuuuuuum :9

⊗

EARPHONES

Earphones are one of the most important things I bring to campus. I'm most productive when I can enjoy my music, so I always have my earphones, and another backup pair in my bag just in case I forget the main ones. My current favorite song right now is Changed by Stanaj.
