  1. Aug 2016: Spike

    Spike is a miniature fairy cactus that I picked up at a farmers market back home. He's small and cute and requires very little care!

  2. Sept 2016: Alfred

    Alfred is an aloe vera plant my mom gave me. He's great for helping out with sunburns, and I keep him on my window sill.

  3. Dec 2016: Jackson

    Jackson is a miniature ball cactus! I got him as a gift, and I love that his pricks are almost fuzzy (though still painfully prickly).

  4. Jan 2016: Vera

    Vera is another breed of aloe vera that grows much thicker and wider. I recently got this little guy, and he's a great compliment to his brother Alfred!
